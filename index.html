meet your coach

Kiley "Coach K" Majeski

25+ years in the circle. Coach K has taken complete beginners through their first correct grip and release — and helped college pitchers at every level sharpen their game.

Her specialty is diagnosis: spotting the real cause of a pitching problem fast, and fixing it. One student pitched with a balance flaw for seven years; Coach K corrected it in a couple of months. Families call her "the wizard" for a reason.

She knows when to push a pitcher and when to guide gently — and she cares about every girl's progress, on and off the field.

FAQ

Where are lessons held?

In person in Huntington Beach, California. The exact location is confirmed with your booking.

What ages do you teach?

Girls roughly 7 and over — from a first-ever lesson to active college pitchers. Every session matches the pitcher's age and level.

My daughter has never pitched. Is that okay?

Absolutely. Coach K regularly starts pitchers from zero — the grip, the throw, and the windmill built correctly from day one.

How fast will we see results?

It depends on the pitcher, but Coach K can often spot and correct the cause of a mechanical problem within a lesson or two. Lasting skill takes consistent practice between lessons.
