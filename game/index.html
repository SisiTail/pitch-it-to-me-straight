Sisi's Run

Sisi is now off the leash and she is hungry. Dodge the walls and snag those bones!

Quick how-to comes first — one tap and you're in.

How to Play

Swipe left / right (or ← → keys) to switch lanes.
Tap (or Space) to jump the low red walls. Tap again mid-air to flip; swipe ↓ to slam back down.
Tall OUT walls can't be jumped — switch lanes! (Show-off move: a double-jump clears them.)
STAY LOW signs hang overhead — do not jump. Stay on the ground and run under them.
Bones = +25. Grab the 🧲 Bone Magnet, ×2 Golden Softball, and Batting Helmet (one free hit).
